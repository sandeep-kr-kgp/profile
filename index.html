Hi, my name is Sandeep and here is some info about me v1 :

The Summary

  • Born on 1st of May, 1997 in a small village near Mathura, UP.
  • At the age of 3, went to a sarkari school, got in a fight with someone and never went back to that school. Got homeschooled for around a year.
  • At the age of 4, went to a private hindi medium UP board school which was located inside the village itself. Got admitted directly in the first class and studied there till class 4th.
  • Switched to an english medium haryana board school in a nearby village. Had to repeat class 4th because I was not up to the English medium standards.
  • 3-4 years were complete shit, didn't like studying at all. For reference, got around 70% in 8th.
  • Got serious about studying because one of the class topper made fun of me. That sparked some kind of fire in me and I became really competitive.
  • Got 96% in 10th. School topper and a record which remains unbroken till now.
  • Got transferred to a CBSE school in a city for 11th and 12th. Stayed in a hostel. Coming from a small village, it took me one year to get acclamatized with the city lifestyle/environment.
  • Went back to being casual and gave up studying. Scored 72% in 11th.
  • Couldn't really muster up the motivation to study and started planning to join defence instead. Towards the end of 12th, for the first time I felt the pressure of what has everyone been expecting from me. Started studying for boards seriously when about 3 months were left. Got 87% somehow.
  • While leaving the school, I was confused and didn't know what to do. One of my friends was going to Kota for JEE and I tagged along.
  • Worked really hard, studied for maybe around 15hrs a day.
  • Cleared JEE, and got admitted to IIT Kharagpur, Mechanical Engineering.
  • Started participating in sports, politics, management etc. Basically everything other than studying engineering.
  • Took up Athletics and stuck to it. Practiced for 2 hrs everyday without skipping a day.
  • Passed out from college and started working as a software engineer.
